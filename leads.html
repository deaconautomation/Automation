Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — who to target

Best Job Titles

  • Head / Director of Operations
  • COO / VP Operations
  • Founder / CEO (small teams)
  • Revenue Ops Manager
  • Chief of Staff
  • Marketing Ops Manager

Best Industries

  • B2B SaaS (Series A–C)
  • Marketing / Creative Agencies
  • E-commerce brands
  • Fintech startups
  • Professional Services
  • Real Estate

Company Sweet Spot

  • 10–200 employees
  • Growing faster than their ops
  • Uses 5+ SaaS tools
  • No dedicated automation engineer
  • $1M–$50M ARR range

Lead With These Pains

  • Manual reporting eating hours
  • Data scattered across tools
  • Repetitive copy-paste between apps
  • Onboarding / offboarding chaos
  • CRM always out of date
  • Too much in spreadsheets

Where to Find Them

  • LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  • Apollo.io (free tier)
  • Twitter / X (ops community)
  • Indie Hackers, Product Hunt
  • Slack communities (Ops, RevOps)
  • Your own network first

Outreach That Works

  • Lead with the specific pain, not the product
  • Mention a tool they likely use
  • Ask one question, not a pitch
  • Follow up 3× before moving on
  • Offer a free 15-min audit call
  • Reference a relevant result / stat
